How It Works

Pipeline Stages

Understanding the analysis process

Stage 1

Frame Extraction

Video is decoded and a uniform frame sequence is extracted. This ensures consistent temporal sampling regardless of video codec or frame rate.

Stage 2

Face Detection & Tracking

Deep neural networks locate and track facial regions across frames, building a continuous identity chain. Misalignments signal potential synthesis.

Stage 3

Artifact Inspection

The system examines texture blends, boundary artifacts, and local reconstruction inconsistencies in facial geometry. Deepfakes often show subtle blending errors.

Stage 4

Authenticity Scoring

Signals from all stages are aggregated into a single confidence score that reflects model certainty about whether the video is authentic or manipulated.

Key Capabilities

Technical strengths

Temporal Analysis

Examines behavior continuity across frame sequences. Synthetic media often exhibits abrupt behavioral shifts or unnatural transitions.

Identity Consistency

Verifies facial features remain stable and geometrically coherent across the video. Generation artifacts often break subtle biometric continuity.

Texture Fidelity

Analyzes skin texture, natural lighting gradients, and micro-expressions. Synthetic faces typically show reduced texture complexity.

Detection Confidence

Understanding result scores

Our confidence score reflects model certainty in its verdict. A high confidence authentic score indicates minimal forensic anomalies. A high confidence deepfake score indicates strong presence of synthesis patterns. No score guarantees absolute truth—it supports informed human decision-making.
